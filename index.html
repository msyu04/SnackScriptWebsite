SnackScript

The enchanting fusion of wizardry and food in programming.

About SnackScript

SnackScript is a spellbinding programming language that blends wizardry with food magic. Designed for readability and fun, it allows developers to cast spells with ingredients rather than traditional syntax.

With SnackScript, loops become "recipes," functions are "spells," and variables are "ingredients." If you've ever wanted to cook up some code, this is the language for you!

Example Programs


# Hello World in SnackScript
whisk "Hello, world!"
        

# Simple loop
recipe stir(3):
    whisk "Mixing..."
        

# Function (spell)
spell bake(cookies):
    if cookies > 0:
        whisk "Baking " + cookies + " cookies!"
    else:
        whisk "No cookies to bake!"
        

# Conditional magic
ingredient sugar = 5
if sugar > 3:
    whisk "Too sweet!"
else:
    whisk "Just right!"
        

# List of ingredients
ingredients pantry = ["flour", "sugar", "eggs"]
whisk pantry[0]  # Outputs: flour
